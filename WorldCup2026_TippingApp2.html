Competition rules
🏆 Tournament Competition
›Open to anyone who registers before the first kickoff on June 12, 2026.
›Register once — your Tournament Winner prediction is locked at registration and cannot be changed.
›Submit match score predictions before each kickoff. Predictions close when a match begins.
›Points accumulate across all 104 matches. Most points after the Final wins.
›Tournament players are automatically entered in the Per Match Competition.
⚽ Per Match Competition
›Open to everyone — no registration required. Just your name to enter.
›Predict the score of any match before kickoff. Enter as many matches as you like.
›Each match has its own leaderboard. Winner announced after the final whistle.
›Join for any match at any time during the tournament.

Wrong result0 pts
Tournament Winner correct10 pts

1Closest total goals predicted — whoever predicted total goals most accurately wins.
2Earliest submission — if still tied, whoever submitted earliest wins.

›All predictions must be submitted before kickoff. Late entries will not be accepted.
›Leaderboards refresh within 60 minutes of full time.
›The venue reserves the right to determine prize winners in any dispute.
›By entering you agree to receive communications from The Crown Hotel. Unsubscribe anytime.
